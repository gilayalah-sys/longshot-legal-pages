Privacy Policy

Program name: Longshot Scanner Alerts · Effective date: July 14, 2026

Longshot Scanner Alerts ("the Service") is an SMS notification program that sends automated stock-market scan alerts to subscribers who have expressly opted in to receive them.

Information We Collect

The Service collects only the mobile phone number a subscriber provides when opting in, along with a record of their consent (date and method of opt-in). No other personal information is collected.

How Information Is Used

Phone numbers are used solely to deliver the SMS alerts the subscriber requested and to honor opt-out (STOP) and help (HELP) requests. Phone numbers are not used for any other purpose.

Data Sharing

No mobile information will be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. Subscriber phone numbers are not sold, rented, or disclosed to any third party. Phone numbers are shared only with our SMS delivery provider (Twilio) as necessary to transmit the messages the subscriber requested.

Data Retention and Deletion

A subscriber's phone number is retained only while their subscription is active. When a subscriber opts out by replying STOP, no further messages are sent to that number.

Opting Out

Reply STOP at any time to stop receiving messages. Reply HELP for help. See the SMS Program Opt-In page for full program details.

Changes to This Policy

Any changes to this policy will be posted on this page.

Contact

Questions about this policy may be directed to the operator of this repository via GitHub.
