Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 12, 2026

This SMS notification service ("the Service") is a personal, private tool operated by an individual for their own use. It sends automated stock-market alert messages exclusively to the operator's own verified phone number.

Information We Collect

The Service does not collect, store, or process any personal information from any third party. The only phone number that receives messages is the operator's own.

Message Recipients

Messages are sent solely to the operator of the Service. No third parties receive messages, and no phone numbers other than the operator's are stored or contacted.

Data Sharing

No personal data is collected, and therefore no personal data is sold, shared, or disclosed to any third party. Mobile information is not shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes.

Opting Out

Reply STOP at any time to stop receiving messages. Reply HELP for help.

Changes to This Policy

Any changes to this policy will be posted on this page.

Contact

Questions about this policy may be directed to the operator of this repository via GitHub.
