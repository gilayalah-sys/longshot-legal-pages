Program name: Longshot Scanner Alerts · Effective date: July 14, 2026

Program Description

Longshot Scanner Alerts is an SMS notification program that delivers automated stock-market scan alerts (ticker symbols, numeric scores, and scan summaries) to subscribers who have expressly opted in to receive them.

Consent

Subscribers consent to receive messages by submitting the opt-in form on the SMS Program Opt-In page and checking the consent box, or by texting START to the service number. Consent is not a condition of any purchase. Subscribers may withdraw consent at any time by replying STOP.

Message Frequency

Message frequency varies based on market conditions and scanner configuration, typically 1–5 messages per trading day. Message and data rates may apply.

Opting Out

Reply STOP to cancel messages at any time. Reply HELP for assistance.
