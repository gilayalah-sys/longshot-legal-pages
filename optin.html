SMS Program Opt-In

Program name: Longshot Scanner Alerts

Program Description

Longshot Scanner Alerts is a personal, single-recipient SMS notification program that delivers automated stock-market scan alerts. The sole recipient of messages is the service owner.

How Consent Is Given

The service owner consents to receive messages by texting START to the service number, and by configuring the service with their own verified phone number as the message recipient.

Message Frequency

Message frequency varies based on market conditions and scanner configuration.

Message and Data Rates

Msg & data rates may apply.

Opting Out / Help

Reply STOP to cancel messages at any time. Reply HELP for assistance.
